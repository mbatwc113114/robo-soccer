Robo Soccer 2025

Compete in the ultimate robotic soccer challenge! Showcase engineering skills and teamwork on the field of intelligent machines.

How It Works

  • Build autonomous robots to play soccer in a competitive arena.
  • Matches consist of two halves, each lasting 15 minutes with real-time robotic plays.
  • Robots must act autonomously during matches; only monitoring is allowed.
  • Judging is based on goals, teamwork, and innovative tech.
  • Bonus awards for design, creativity, and sportsmanship.
